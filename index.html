Hi! I'm a Master’s student at Middlebury College in Paris working on a thesis about climate change politics in the Pacific while interning at the Delegation of Palau to UNESCO. I've served two years as a community development volunteer with the US Peace Corps in Chiang Rai, Thailand, and I hold a BA cum laude in Anthropology and French from Dartmouth College.
